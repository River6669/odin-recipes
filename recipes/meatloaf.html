Meatloaf

Description

This is a traditional meatloaf recipe. It is very basic and you can add your own flair to it very easily. Feel free to substitute a portion of the ground beef for any other ground meat you may wish.

Meatloaf is an easy and filling meal that is beloved by most. This includes a recipe for a ketchup sauce to go on top that adds a sweet and tangy crust to your meatloaf.

Ingredients

Meatloaf

  • 2LBS Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion, diced and caramelized
  • 3TBSP Ketchup
  • 3/4cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/3cup milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • Season to Taste

Sauce

  • 3/4cup Ketchup
  • 1 1/2tsp White Vinegar
  • 2 1/2TBSP Brown Sugar, can be light or dark
  • 1tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2tsp Onion Powder
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

You should make the meatloaf first and then the sauce to put on top.

Meatloaf

  1. Add ground beef, caramelized onion, ketchup, breadcrumbs, milk and eggs to a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add seasonings to taste. I recommend salt, pepper, garlic, and italian seasoning
  3. Use hands to thoroughly mix the ingredients together.
  4. Press mixture into a 9" x 5" loaf pan

Sauce

  1. Whisk together ketchup and white vinegar
  2. Add brown sugar, garlic and onion powder, and salt and pepper
  3. Whisk together

Finishing Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Pour ketchup mixture over meatloaf
  3. Cover with foil
  4. Bake for 45 mins to an hour, removing foil after 30 mins
  5. Let rest for 15 mins before cutting and serving

There you have it, a delicious meatloaf that pairs well with mashed potatoes and most vegetables
