Dinner Rolls

Description

These classic dinner rolls are buttery and fluffy. THey come together easily and are a hit among every family member, no matter how picky. They can be eaten on your own or used as slider buns. Perfect for hand held sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 3cups flour
  • 1TBSP yeast
  • 1/3cup sugar
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1cup warm milk
  • 1/3cup room temperature butter, cubed

Steps

  1. Whisk together flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a large bowl
  2. Add egg and warm milk slowly to flour mixture, mix until loose dough
  3. Fold in softened butter
  4. Knead dough for at least 10 minutes
  5. Cover and proof for 10 minutes
  6. Divide dough into 12 equal portions and roll into balls
  7. Cover and proof for 30 minutes, or until doubled
  8. Bake at 350°F for 12-15 minutes until golden brown
  9. Brush with melted butter and let cool
