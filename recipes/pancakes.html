Pancakes

Description

These classic pancakes are perfectly fluffy. Try them as is or with fun add-ins. My favorites are cinnamon, chocolate chips, or bananas.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2cups flour
  • 2TBSP sugar
  • 1TBSP baking powder
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1 1/4cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 4TBSP melted butter, with extra for skillet
  • 1tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. whisk together dry ingredients
  2. warm milk to room temp and melt butter
  3. whisk wets together until combined
  4. make a well in flour mix and use a fork to mix together, do not overmix
  5. let sit for 5 mins

Cooking Instructions

  1. heat a skillet to a medium heat
  2. lightly brush with butter
  3. pour approximately 1/3cup batter into warmed skillet
  4. flip once after bubbles on the surface pop
  5. cook other side for 15-30 seconds or until golden brown
