Pretzel Caramel Pecan Treat

Description

These sweet and savory treats are great for any occasion. Bite sized, transportable, and storable at room temperature, they are a great choice for any party or gift bag.

Ingredients

  • Pretzels
  • Bag of Rolos
  • Pecans, shelled

Steps

  1. Place pretzels individually onto a cookie sheet
  2. Place 1 Rolo on each pretzel
  3. Bake in a 300°F oven
  4. Remove from oven once Rolos have melted, approximately 10 mins
  5. Place pecan on to each melted Rolo to stick
  6. Let cool and eat
