Thin Pizza Crust

Description

A thin crust that can be used to make any pizza you can imagine. From a basic pepperoni to BBQ chicken, this crust is versatile and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2tsp dry yeast
  • 1/2tsp white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups water, 110-115°F
  • 4cups flour
  • 1tsp salt
  • extra seasonings to taste, I recommend onion and garlic powder

Steps

  1. proof yeast with sugar and warm water for 10-15 minutes
  2. whisk seasonings and flour together in a large bowl
  3. slowly add yeast mixture to flour and combine
  4. knead dough together for 10 minutes until smooth and holds together well
  5. gently roll and stretch to fit your pizza pan, or divide into smaller personal pizzas
  6. top with sauce and toppings, let your imagination run wild, or stay classic, it's your choice
