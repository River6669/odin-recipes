Naan

Description

Soft, fluffy and flavorful, naan is the perfect side to many dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2cup warm water 110-115°F
  • 2tsp yeast
  • 1tsp sugar
  • 3TBSP olive oil
  • 2TBSP milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 2 1/2cups all-purpose flour
  • 2tsp minced garlic
  • 2TBSP melted butter

Steps

  1. proof yeast in warm water with sugar for 10-15 minutes
  2. in the same bowl mix together olice oil, milk, and egg
  3. in a separate bowl, whisk together flour and salt
  4. slowly add wet mixture to flour while combining
  5. knead together until smooth
  6. separate into 8 equal portions, roll into balls, and flatten
  7. in a warmed skillet cook evenly on both sides until fluffy
  8. brush finished naan with melted butter and garlic
