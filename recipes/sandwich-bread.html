Sandwich Bread

Description

A delicious and fluffy white bread, perfect for sandwiches and toast, if it lasts that long. It will be tempting to eat it with your hands straight out of the pan.

Ingredients

  • 1cup warm water, between 98°F and 115°F
  • 2 1/4tsp dry yeast
  • 1/3cup white granulated sugar
  • 2TBSP oil or butter
  • 3cups bread flour
  • 3/4tsp salt

note If you do not have bread flour you can use all-purpose, however it will have a very different texture to most sandwich breads. This is why I reccomend bread flour.

Steps

  1. Proof yeast in warm water with 1tsp sugar for 10 minutes. It should be foamy
  2. Add oil and the rest of the sugar to your yeast mixture.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine your bread flour and salt
  4. Add your yeast mixture to your flour and combine until a ball is formed
  5. Knead your dough for 10 minutes
  6. Proof for 60-90 minutes or until doubled in size
  7. Flatten, stretch, and roll dough, place into a greased bread pan.
  8. Proof for 60-90 minutes until doubled in size
  9. Preheat your oven to 350°F
  10. Bake for 30-35 minutes until top is golden brown
  11. Let rest upside down until cool before cutting for best results

This recipe can be used as a base for other breads very easily. Experiment on your own with different flavorings and inclusions. You will be suprised by what you can come up with.
