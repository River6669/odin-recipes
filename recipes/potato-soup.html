Potato Soup

Description

This creamy potato soup is a hit with the whole family. It comes together easily and is a staple at my house.

Ingredients

  • 3 strips bacon
  • 1 1/2TBSP butter
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1-2TBSP flour
  • 1lb potatoes, diced
  • 2cups chicken broth
  • 1cup milk
  • 1/2cup carrots, round sliced
  • seasonings to taste, I reccomend salt, pepper, cajun

Steps

  1. cook bacon in pot, remove and leave fat in pan
  2. add butter, onion, and carrot to pot, cook until tender
  3. add garlic, simmer until fragrant
  4. sprinkle flour, stir until smooth
  5. add all ingredients except bacon, stir well
  6. bring to a boil, cook until potatoes are tender
  7. mash half of potatoes, return to pot, alternatively you could use an immersion blender
  8. add seasonings to taste, simmer 10-15 minutes
  9. add topping of choice and serve, I reccomend sour cream and cheese.
