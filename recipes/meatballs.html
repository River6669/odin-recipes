Meatballs

Description

These meatballs in sauce come together quickly for a fun dinner or potluck side.

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1/4cup panko
  • 1 egg
  • seasonings to taste, I use parsley, nutmeg, onion, garlic, salt and pepper

Sauce

  • 3TBSP butter
  • 3TBSP flour
  • 1 1/2cup beef broth
  • 1/2cup milk
  • worchestershire sauce or soy sauce
  • mustard

Steps

  1. mix ground beef, panko, seasonings and egg together
  2. roll mixed beef into 1TBSP balls, set aside
  3. in a large pan, melt butter on low-medium heat
  4. slowly whisk in flour until combined and bubbly
  5. add beef broth and milk slowly to rue until mixed, turn to simmer
  6. add meatballs, worchestershire or soy sauce, and mustard to pan
  7. cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes

These meatballs are good on their own or over pasta, garnish with parsley for an elevated feel
