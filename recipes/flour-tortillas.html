Description

Tortillas are a staple in every home. They are quick and easy to make and can last in the fridge for a few days. From taco tuesday to a sweet treat tortillas are versatile and the uses are only limited to your imagination.

Ingredients

  • 3cups flour
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 1/3cup neutral oil or butter
  • 1cup warm water

Steps

  1. preheat a pan to medium high
  2. mix all ingredients in a large bowl until a dough is formed
  3. knead dough until smooth, about 10-15 minutes
  4. separate into 16 equal portions and roll into balls
  5. flatten each ball and roll or stretch until it fits the pan
  6. place flattened dough one at a time into the pan, cook for 15-30 seconds on each side
