Biscuits

Description

These fluffy, buttery biscuits are perfect for breakfast as well as dinner. Pair with gravy for a filling snack.

Ingredients

  • 2cups all-purpose flour
  • 1TBSP baking powder
  • 1TBSP sugar
  • 1tsp salt
  • 6TBSP butter, frozen and grated
  • 3/4cup milk

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 425°F and grease a baking pan
  2. combine dry ingredients
  3. add frozen butter and combine until has the appearance of bread crumbs
  4. add milk and combine to form a loose dough
  5. knead until just cohesive
  6. roll out onto floured surface
  7. fold 5-6 times, flatten until 1" thick
  8. cut using a biscut cutter or large glass
  9. bake at 425°F for 12 minutes or until tops are golden
  10. brush tops with melted butter and serve
